Tim Daoust

Tim Daoust

Tim is contemporary painter who specialises in landscapes, portraits, and still life painting. He studied computer graphics and engineering in the United States and worked in the video game industry for 12 years. He then trained in the Classical Atelier method at London Fine Art studios for six years, where he now teaches on the weekends. When not painting he works in the visual effects industry producing technology for feature films.

Tim takes portrait commisions from his studio in Battersea, London. Charcoal prices start at £500 and oil portraits start at £1000.
